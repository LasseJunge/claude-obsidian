# Scraping German real-estate data — legal notes

- **Public listing facts** (rent, m², rooms, location) — generally lawful to scrape at reasonable rates in Germany.
- **Terms of Service** — most portals (incl. ImmoScout24) prohibit scraping; breach is a contractual/civil risk, not criminal.
- **GDPR** — the hard boundary. Agent/realtor **names and contact details are PII**; commercial use needs a legal basis (consent or legitimate interest). Do not store EU-citizen PII without one.
- **Practical rules:** don't scrape faster than the site can handle; don't scrape non-public data; strip/avoid PII.

> [!gap] Commercial use of scraped data → seek German legal counsel. This note is orientation, not legal advice.
